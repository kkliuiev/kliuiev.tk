QA Start

  • Understanding what software testing is and what place it takes in the software development life cycle.
  • Skills of working with the main software products used in the workflow.
  • You will learn how client-server applications work and what are the specifics of testing such applications.
  • Check out the various software development methodologies (including the most popular Scrum today).
  • The ability to create and maintain technical documentation.
  • Familiarize yourself with the classification of the main types and levels of testing.
  • Get hands-on experience in testing various applications.
  • Go through a test interview and be prepared for successful employment and a trial period.
